Spirited Away

In this animated film by renowned Japanese director Hayao Miyazaki, a 10-year-old girl named Chihiro and her parents discover an apparently abandoned amusement park. When her parents are transformed into huge pigs, Chihiro encounters Haku, a mysterious figure who reveals that the park serves as a retreat for supernatural creatures seeking respite from the human world. To rescue herself and her parents, Chihiro must labor at the park.
